Privacy Policy

(Updated by XIKE LABS : 5-5-2025)

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Technical Information: This includes device type, operating system, screen resolution, device language, country code, and advertising ID (IDFA/GAID). Such data helps us optimize the game for different devices and operating systems.
  • Usage Data: We track play frequency, levels completed, interactions, and playtime. This information allows us to understand user interaction and improve the game experience.
  • Crash Data: Error reports and app crashes are monitored to quickly detect and fix issues, ensuring smooth gameplay.
  • Remote Configurations: This feature allows us to adjust game content in real-time, providing the best possible experience for players.

2. Children and User Audience

Our game is suitable for all ages but is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We encourage parents to supervise their children's online activities to ensure a safe gaming environment.

3. Third-Party Services & SDKs Used

We utilize third-party SDKs to enhance user experience and manage advertising:

  • Firebase Analytics: This tool tracks player behavior to help us optimize the game.
  • Crashlytics: It provides error reporting, enabling us to resolve issues swiftly.
  • Firebase Remote Config: This service updates game parameters in real-time to ensure the best user experience.
  • Google AdMob, AppLovin MAX, IronSource, Facebook Audience Network, etc.: These platforms help us effectively display and manage ads.

All SDKs comply with GDPR, COPPA, and current regulations to protect user privacy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to better understand how your data is used.

4. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to improve the game, analyze performance, and display ads that are relevant to users.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties outside of the listed SDKs. All information is secured and used solely for the purposes stated in this policy.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Data Retention

Data is stored for the necessary duration to serve the stated purposes and will be deleted when no longer needed.

8. Security

We implement advanced security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access and ensure data integrity.

9. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Users are encouraged to check regularly to stay informed of the latest information.

10. Contact
